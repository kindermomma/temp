About Amanda

Hi, I’m so happy you’re here! I’m Amanda, a kindergarten teacher by day and a mama to two wild and silly boys by night.

I graduated from Ball State University with a Bachelors of Arts in Elementary Education with a concentration in Kindergarten/Primary Education. My first two years I taught Kindergarten in a low income school where over half of my students did not speak English. This is where I quickly learned the importance of differentiation and how to meet the needs of all learners.

Since then, I have moved, and had two babies. Being a teacher mama is tough to balance but it’s helped me become a more empathic teacher. My passion is to create a playful classroom
